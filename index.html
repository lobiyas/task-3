Black coffee

Black coffee leaves a bitter taste in the mouth. Have a black coffee - that should sober you up! Black coffee is just coffee that hasn't had any additions added to it, such as sugar, milk, cream, or flavorings. Many individuals enjoy a strong cup of black coffee, even if it has a little bitter taste compared to coffee flavored with additions. It is, in fact, a regular element of some people's diet.





Filter coffee

Indian filter coffee is a coffee drink made by mixing hot milk and sugar with the infusion obtained by percolation brewing of finely ground coffee powder with chicory in a traditional Indian filter. It has been described as "hot, strong, sweet and topped with bubbly froth" and is known as filter kaapi in India.
